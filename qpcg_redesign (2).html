About QPCG

Founded in 2015 to push the boundaries of what a student-led organization can achieve

The Queen's Private Capital Group is a student-run organization that provides financial advisory services to private equity and venture capital firms across North America.

We strive to be recognized by students and finance professionals across Canada as the top extracurricular opportunity for those interested in private markets.
